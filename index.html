Shiba Inu

The Basics

Shiba inus are a dog breed from Japan that was originally used for hunting. Now they are growing increasingly popular as household pets, thanks to their popularity on the internet from memes like Doge. They are commonly mistaken as foxes due to their spiked ears, orangey fur, and slim faces.

Temperament

Independent, somewhat aggressive, loving, strong prey drive, bold, intelligent

Other names

Doge, shibe, shobe
